Hi, I'm Dropped Out.

Dropped Out is the moniker of Mumbai based electronic artist, Rishi Thakker who was recently named one of the Top 5 Artists to look out for in 2020 by Loudest.in. The 21 year old has received support from major publications like Rolling Stone India, Wild City, Bangin Beats, Stereofox & Wild Patterns among others and cosigns from artists like San Holo. His track 'Bipolar' just crossed 200k plays on Spotify and it was a part of the Lakme Fashion Week campaign in India and was also featured in a video by popular YouTuber, Nightscape which has amassed over 2.6 Million views. His latest collaboration “Azaad Hu Mein'' with Rapper, Yashraj ended up on two major Spotify Editorial playlists in India namely, “New Music Hindi” & “hIndies” and garnered amazing support from the likes of Bobby Friction.
