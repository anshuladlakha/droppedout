Hi, I'm Dropped Out.

Spending hours and hours locked in his room making music, Rishi wasn’t really the average Indian joe growing up. The 21 year old has spent the last 7 years of his life making FL Studio the most familiar sight for him to look at. As suggested by his moniker “Dropped Out”, the Mumbai based artist decided to focus solely on music instead of finishing grad school and since then he has been able to channel all his energy into making music that seeks to create an atmosphere for the listener to float around in while feeling and comprehending all that the producer intends them to feel. In 2020, Rishi’s personal goal was to challenge his limits and include his own vocals in his tracks which he accomplished by releasing the song called “Daze” in January. His next song “Home” is a downtempo track which comes out on 5th March and while it deviates a little from his usual sound, it sets the tone for his upcoming projects.
